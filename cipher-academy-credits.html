Inspirations

Cipher Academy is inspired by tools I deeply admire, especially in how they approach learning and progression.

  • Morse Mania — Dong Digital: A great reference for structured learning and game design, which influenced parts of Cipher Academy's interface and level system. While Cipher Academy explores multiple encoding systems and how they can be combined (e.g. for escape games), Morse Mania is an excellent choice to go deeper into Morse code. Morse Mania on Google Play

  • Braille Academy — Dong Digital: Its clear interface and progression system helped shape parts of Cipher Academy. If you want to go further with Braille beyond the basics, it's a fantastic resource. Braille Academy on Google Play
