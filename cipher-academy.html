You've seen cipher puzzles in escape rooms, spy movies, and puzzle hunts. Cipher Academy is how you actually learn to read them — fast.

Pick a cipher. Start with single letters. Build up to full words. Each one has its own progression, its own challenges, and its own way to test you.

How it works

Every cipher follows a carefully designed learning path. Morse starts with E and T. Braille follows its historical decades. Pigpen traces the geometry of its grid. A spaced repetition engine schedules your reviews so symbols stick in long-term memory.
