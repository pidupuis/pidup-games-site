Privacy Policy

Goose Academy is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected
  • No data is stored on external servers or transmitted over the network
  • No tracking or analytics tools are used
  • No internet connection is required to play

All gameplay data (progress, settings) is stored entirely on your device using local storage. We have no access to it.

Permissions

The app requires only two permissions:

  • Internet: required only for in-app purchases via Google Play
  • Billing: required to process the one-time full version purchase

The app does not access your microphone, camera, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device feature.

In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase the full version, the transaction is handled entirely by the platform (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. There are no accounts, no forms, and no network requests.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The only third-party interaction is the Google Play billing service for in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
